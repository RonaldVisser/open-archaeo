A list of open source archaeological software and resources

Chronophage Jan 1, 0001 Applications for obtaining chronograms (quantified chronology diagrams) and time distribution curves of archaeological or historical units from quantified dating indicators treated as inaccuracy intervals.
Classical and ancient Linked Open Data Jan 1, 0001 A list of linked open data repositories relating to classics and ancient history.
CloudCal Jan 1, 0001 This app will allow you to build & apply calibrations for the Tracer & Artax series of XRF devices
clustering_14C Jan 1, 0001 A python implementation of the method of clustering radiocarbon dates in order to determine whether they represent separate events, or phases in time
coenocliner Jan 1, 0001 An R package to simulate species occurences and abundances counts, along gradient.
coproID Jan 1, 0001 CoproID helps you to identify the “true maker” of Illumina sequenced Coprolites/Paleofaeces by checking the microbiome composition and the endogenous DNA.
Crossbones Jan 1, 0001 Crossbones autogenerates 3D schematic skeletons, making rapid surveying and visualisation of dense skeletal assemblages a snap.
crp2a Jan 1, 0001 Dose Rate Estimation from In-Situ Gamma-Ray Spectrometry Measurements for Luminescence Dating
ctv-archaeology Jan 1, 0001 This CRAN Task View contains a list of packages useful for scientific work in Archaeology
CuneiPainter Jan 1, 0001 An App to recognize cuneiform characters on your Android phone.
